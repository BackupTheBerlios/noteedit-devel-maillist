Hi all,

tonight I've committed almost the complete fix for bug 2807 (rests with 
fermatas).

NoteEdit now supports editing rests with fermatas and writing these 
in .abc, .not, .ly (untested), .tex (untested) and .xml format. Writing 
in .pmx format is not supported. Reading in .xml format is supported.

As I am not familiar with lex and yacc, it would cost me a significant amount 
time to also implement reading in .not format. Could someone else please do 
that ?

The small print: I don't use either MusixTeX or LilyPond, thus the 
implementation is untested and may be incorrect. Please report any problems. 
Furthermore, I (arbitrarily) decided to support fermatas only on normal rests 
and not on multirests.

Enjoy, Leon.
