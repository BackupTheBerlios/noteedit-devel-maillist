No, Lily wouldn't accept it (I tested with 2.4.2 and it doesn't work). 
Above, I only tried to explain how the notation usually looks nowadays 
(LilyPond output actually). But for Lily input, it's necessary to insert 
exact values (that is r2. (half dotted rest) for full measure length 
rest in 3/4 beat). I'm not aware of the NoteEdit bug, so I apologize for 
the misconception.

That would be a good idea. I'm working on a project of mine (still at 
blueprints and numerous tests, but thinking a lot:)) meant as a perfect 
composition tool. I've taken the concept of staves, lyrics etc. from 
LilyPond and I think it is the best around (comparing to Finale or the
