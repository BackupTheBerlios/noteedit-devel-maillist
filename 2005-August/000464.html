Well I had many recommendation for Design with the book "Effectice C++".
This one covers the basic program design matters, and an second one
"More Effective C++" the more advanced themes. Also the STL (Standard
Templace Library) is covered in an extra book. General design books
concerning software projects should be based on UML-2. I have two
books, but they are both in german (basics and advanced topics).
Maybe UML-2-Toolkit is a book for you if you want to learn more
about software analysis and design. The UML-Bible on the contrary
is a book covering everything about UML, for those who want to get
an UML expert. I want to buy a new book less covering UML itself but
concentrating on the software design process. Maybe the book
"Object-Oriented Analysis and Design : Understanding System Development 
with UML 2.0" helps you if you want to take a similar approach.
And finally you might be interested in Interface design books...
