LilyPond team yesterday released a new development 2.7.5 version, which
brought a new major feature IMO - text scripts (along with lyrics) now
detect vertical&horizontal collision with other music elements (slurs,
noteheads, other markings). Also, if the sillable in lyrics is to wide
and doesn't fit, the space between notes is extended, to fit.

I just thought I might tell you that as I usually had many problems with
fine-tuning text markings in Lily document, but now it's all
automatically. I don't need to use #padding anymore!:)
