>Some words about my plan:
>
>The music elements should be placed into a linked list, which have
>'next' and 'prev' methods and inherits from QObject. This would be the
>base of the new architecture. 
>
>If an element requires the clef for example, just walks through the
>'prev' methods, till finds it. 
>
>If a new Clef is inserted, clefChanged(NClef *) signal will be emitted
>for all affected items in the linked list (the clefs are cached).
>
>With an architecture like that, quite high code separation could be
>reached.
>
>The current architecture is very very far from it. That's why I wrote,
>that it requires half year or more to finish.
