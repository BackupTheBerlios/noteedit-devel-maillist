The first plan I had in mind was to use Alt key modifier for fast scrolling.

I took a peak in different apps for mouse wheel behaviour. Here are
actions for modifiers used with mouse scroll:
OpenOffice.org2: 0-VScroll, CTRL-zoom
Inkscape: 0-VScroll, SHIFT-HScroll, CTRL-zoom
GIMP: 0-VScroll, SHIFT-zoom, CTRL-HScroll
Blender: 0-zoom, SHIFT-VScroll, CTRL-HScroll

There are probably different behaviours for the combination of them as
well, but I didn't test it. IMO, combinations in NoteEdit shouldn't mean
any special behaviours, but only combined actions.
NoteEdit: 0-HScroll, SHIFT-VScroll, CTRL-zoom, ALT-fast HScroll,
ALT+SHIFT-fast VScroll, ALT+CTRL-fast zoom(?), SHIFT+CTRL+ALT=crash;)
