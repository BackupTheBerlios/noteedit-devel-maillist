> I would like to assign the following behaviours to the scroll wheel:
> - - Ordinary scroll wheel - Horizontal scrollbar
> - - SHIFT + scroll wheel - Vertical scrollbar
> - - CTRL + scroll wheel - Zoom in/out
> - - ALT + scroll wheel - Fast pan
> 
> I think the current assignment for setting note's pitch is pretty
> useless IMO. What do you think?

You haven't considered all the possibilities of wheel devices.
You can buy mice with both vertical and horizontal scrolling and
you can even buy devices with complete free scrolling (like my
own mouse :-). You only need to change the XOrg.conf settings
so that this kind of scrolling works.

So I would at least add:
- Ordinary scroll wheel(s) - Horizontal, Vertical and diagonal scrolling

I've read this in the Wiki too, but I'm faster writing E-Mails ;-)
