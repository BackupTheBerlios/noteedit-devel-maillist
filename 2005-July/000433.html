Hi,

for me SuSE 9.3 is very stable. On 9.2 I had occasionaly freezes, which
was not connected to NoteEdit, and for which I blamed the ATI issues. Is
it only X that freezes?

Did you compile and install NoteEdit properly, so you don't have a mix
of the original rpm and the self compiled?  You can play midi files with
the tse3 scheduler:

     tse3play -alsa -port <number>:0 <name>.mid

I do the configuration like this:

    ./configure --prefix=/opt/kde3 --libdir=/opt/kde3/lib64
--with-qt-libraries=/usr/lib/qt3/lib64 \
                --disable-final --with-libtse3-libs=/usr/lib64

When the rlimit/realtime issues are solved, very soon I think, there is
now reason to look back to kernel 2.4.

Let us know,
Georg
