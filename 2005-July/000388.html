What was wrong? Simple! Even stupid...
After installing tse3-0.3.0 the configure script of NoteEdit didn't find
the library and I didn't recognice the false library name. So I tried
to fix this by adding the 

--with-libtse3-include=/usr/include/tse3/ --with-libtse3-libs=/usr/lib/

to configure script. Even this failed. Then you gave me the hint about
the false library name. But I (stupid) still tried to call configure with
these extra options...

Simply remove these solved the problem. And now I know that the
include path for tse3 is NOT /usr/include/tse3/ but /usr/include/

I just installed NoteEdit-2.7.4 successfully and will play with it now...
