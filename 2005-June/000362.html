Hi,

I created a first version for a LinuxTag 2005 flyer (see attachment for
files). It is based on the Logo from the splash screen and the icon
both converted to BW with gimp. (If you don't have the svg's any
longer and need them just drop me a note).

Most of the text is from the README and doesn't need to be changed.
I added a "Help Us" section and expanded Homepage & Mailing list under
a "Get in Contact" headline. If you have better ideas to improve
this just go ahead and mail it (or change the document directly).

I've also added some information how to get noteedit source and
binaries as well as some hints what is in the noteedit archive.

I'm also restarting work on the poster after I get back from a
bicycle ride (in one or two hours).

Linuxtag 2005 preparation is costing much time so there was no
time left for me doing something on NoteEdit. The release is
still pending and I wouldn't disagree if someone could bring
it out even if the crash bug at start still isn't fixed (I
didn't have the time to look for that as well).
