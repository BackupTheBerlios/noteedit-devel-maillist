Hi,

I uploaded rc1 to Berlios. For this release I created a makefile
that makes it more easy to create the release. I attached it
to the mail.
"make release" creates the archive from a freshly checked out
svn repository.
"make testrelease" unpacks the archive and creates the noteedit
binary. You still have to do a manual "make install" from the
noteedit directory as this requires root privileges.

I will upload another archive with the fresh patch from
raphael in about half an hour, so the officially announcement
is delayed until then.
