Hello to all!

I spotted another 2 show-stopper bugs and I advice to fix them before
the final release:
- Lyrics are interpreted as HTML text. Sometimes, < and > signs (used in
NE for syllabic singing) mess up things really bad. The HTML parsing
should be disabled somehow in the textBox widget.
- I got a crash when deleting a tied-over-bar note sometimes in non 1st
voice - have to investigate further

I'm planning to fix these bugs till this Sunday, so no rush for the
release just yet :)

Regards.
- Matevž
