Hi,

Somewhere it's documented, somewhere under SVN. But shortly, let's say,
if your personal berlios username (unixname) is 'atberlios', you have to
do those two commands:

    export SVN_SSH="ssh -l atberlios"
    svn checkout svn+ssh://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/noteedit

To work with berlios is great!
