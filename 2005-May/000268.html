Hi all,

when a note or chord is inserted and there is not enough room left in
the current bar NoteEdit places the rest of it nicely in the next bar
and ties it, as it should be. I made a small patch that makes my life
easier: When the part before bar sign consists itself of a shorter and a
longer value, NoteEdit now flushes the longer part in second place, just
before the bar sign. I think, in all cases this gives a better rhythmic
notation. So no corrections by hand in this case any more. If I oversaw
something, please let it know.

There are issues with auto bar insertion when placing 8 beat notes.
Maybe I'll look at those soon.
