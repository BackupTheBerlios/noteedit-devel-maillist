ok, it's really wrong as it were on the homepage... now, that's 
probably to your unlucky repository layout (you should put what's 
called "CVS HEAD" in the CVS world into /trunk in the SVN world). 
Since no trunk was created, you have the following URL:

svn checkout svn://svn.berlios.de/noteedit/

Whereas here on the web (see 
http://developer.berlios.de/svn/?group_id=2232) it says:

svn checkout svn://svn.berlios.de/noteedit/trunk


I have downloaded it now.

Note that you will have a hard time creating branches if you do not 
use /trunk or something in your repository for your main line of 
development. Branches in Subversion are just a different path, 
like /branches/noteedit-2 or /georg/make-it-cool-branch
whatever :)

... I don't know if you need instructions for subversion or not, but 
in case you do I know a little bit about it. But I don't want to 
sound like I knew everything.

Just in case you need more info, there is the complete subversion 
handbook: http://svnbook.red-bean.com/
