NoteEdit does use the standard KDE dialog for keyboard shortcuts.
It calls KKeyDialog::configureKeys with the keys defined in keys_
(see mainframewidget.cpp). Can't say anything about the icon.
At least the menu entry is no StdAction of KDE. I don't even know
if there is a StdAction for configureKeys in KDE.
