I changed the Makefile.am in the icons directory.
When installing Noteedit an error occured when
installing it. Reason is that $datadir references
$kde_datadir and this variable again references
$datadir. I changed $datadir to $(prefix)/share/apps.

I know it's not the best solution in case $kde_datadir
changes one day but I didn't find other way around
the recursion problem.
