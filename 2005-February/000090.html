Hi all,

The tarball looks good: It builds on a Suse 9.0 (kernel 2.4.21, kde 3.1) 
with just

      tar xvfz <tarball>
      cd noteedit-2-7-4
      configure --prefix=/opt/kde3
      make

as described in INSTALL.

On my Suse 9.2 x86-64 (kernel 2.6.8, kde 3.3) things look also okay: The 
Suse spec-skript/-patch is still needed. Thus to make a Suse rpm someone 
needs just replace 2.7.3-tarball with the new one.
