Hi Reinhard,

I am not especially looking forward to the job of release technician, just 
trying to push 2.7.4 out of the door. I am perfectly happy with you making 
the release (even happier then when I would have to do it :-)).

Apparently, I was not clear enough about my intentions: I also made a tarball 
tonight. Doesn't matter, but let's try to avoid wasting time like this. Once 
again, I am perfectly happy with your version. Feel free to test and release 
it. In case you run into problems, I also have a version available that at 
least builds and works OK on Fedora Core 3. Let me know if you need it.

This is what I did:
unzip/untar noteedit-2.7.3.tgz
- note: be careful to check the five links in admin !
mv noteedit-2.7.3 noteedit-2.7.4-beta1
replace following files by svn versions:
- AUTHORS
- FAQ
- FAQ.de
- INSTALL
- INSTALL.de
- README
- VERSION
- doc/de/index.docbook
- doc/noteedit/index.docbook
- noteedit/* (all updated files)
- noteedit/po/Makefile.in
- noteedit/po/sl.po
note: don't copy:
- Makefile.cvs
- entire admin directory
edit 2.7.3 into 2.7.4 in:
- configure.in.in
- configure.in
- configure

If you need help in testing your tarball, I would be happy to do that too. 
Available platforms are Core 3 and RedHat 7.2 (old, but 2.7.3 still works on 
it). Just tell me where to download it from.

Regards, Leon.
