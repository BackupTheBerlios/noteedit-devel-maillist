Something like:
$ svn copy svn+ssh://ckarai@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/noteedit/trunk
svn+ssh://ckarai@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/noteedit/branches/csaba_coderewrite2

should directly do the trick. You will get a new revision number then
and on the next svn update, local sources will get updated as well.

Whenever you are going to apply your changes to the mainstream trunk,
you should use:
$svn merge -r <the_revision_number,when_your_branch_was_created>:HEAD
svn+ssh://ckarai@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/noteedit/branches/csaba_coderewrite2
svn+ssh://ckarai@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/noteedit/trunk

This will make a diff from revision
<the_revision_number,when_your_branch_was_created> to the latest one of
/csaba_coderewrite2, and apply that diff to the /trunk.

And if you want to delete your branch some day (when the merge is
complete), use:
$svn delete
svn+ssh://ckarai@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/noteedit/branches/csaba_coderewrite2
